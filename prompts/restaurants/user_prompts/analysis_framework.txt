**ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK FOR RESTAURANT REVIEW:**

TARGET ASPECT: {target}

STEP 1 - ASPECT-SPECIFIC EVALUATION:
- Focus specifically on comments about: {target}
- Separate from overall restaurant evaluation
- Consider domain expectations for this aspect

STEP 2 - SENTIMENT SIGNAL DETECTION:
- Identify explicit praise/criticism about {target}
- Detect implicit signals: irony, understatement, professional assessment
- Evaluate emotional tone and intensity

STEP 3 - CONTEXTUAL INTERPRETATION:
- Consider the review's overall tone
- Understand cultural/dining expectations
- Assess reviewer sophistication level

STEP 4 - CONFIDENCE ASSESSMENT:
- High confidence: Clear, consistent signals about {target}
- Medium confidence: Mixed or mild signals
- Low confidence: Ambiguous or contradictory indicators

STEP 5 - FINAL DECISION:
- Choose Positive: Clear satisfaction with {target}
- Choose Negative: Clear dissatisfaction with {target}
- Choose Neutral: Balanced, factual, or ambivalent about {target}
